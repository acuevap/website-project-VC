Alejandro Cueva - Founding Partner

With years of experience in the financial services industry in Latin America, Alejandro has the ability to not only identify businesses with the potential to disrupt the industry and improve the relationship between people and banks, but also to make the connections that will help founders get there.

Alejandro graduated with a BA in International Finance from Tecnologico de Monterrey, completed a Master’s in Finance at EGADE Business School, and has an MBA from Georgetown University’s McDonough School of Business.
